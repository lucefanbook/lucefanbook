# The Rosary of Joyful Mysteries (Monday and Saturday)

## Introduction

Praying the Joyful Mysteries on the Rosary involves meditating on five key events from the early life of Jesus Christ and the Virgin Mary. These mysteries are traditionally prayed on Mondays and Saturdays, as well as during the Advent season.
